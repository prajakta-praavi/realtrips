Escape to the Andaman Islands, a tropical paradise of pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush green landscapes. This journey takes you through the capital city Port Blair, the serene Havelock Island, and the untouched beauty of Neil Island — a perfect getaway for nature lovers, beach enthusiasts, and adventure seekers.

Port Blair (3 Nights)
Start your island adventure in Port Blair, the gateway to the Andaman Islands. Visit the historic Cellular Jail, explore the serene Corbyns Cove Beach, and enjoy the tranquil waters. A visit to Chidiya Tapu will offer spectacular sunset views, while Ross Island takes you through the remnants of British colonial history.

Havelock Island (2 Nights)
Head to Havelock Island, famous for its white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. Relax at the beautiful Radhanagar Beach, rated one of Asia’s best beaches, and indulge in water activities like snorkeling, scuba diving, and kayaking. The serene surroundings make it the perfect spot for relaxation and adventure.

Neil Island (1 Night)
A short boat ride will take you to Neil Island, known for its untouched beaches and vibrant coral reefs. Spend a day in this tranquil haven, with its laid-back atmosphere perfect for a peaceful escape. Explore the pristine Laxmanpur Beach and Natural Bridge before heading back to Port Blair.
