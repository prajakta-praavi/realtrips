A perfect mix of hill stations, wildlife, backwaters, and beaches, this tour is an escape into the serene landscapes and vibrant culture of South India.

Munnar – The Tea Garden Retreat
Begin your trip in Munnar, where the lush green tea gardens and misty mountains set a tranquil atmosphere. Explore the sprawling tea estates, visit Mattupetty Dam, and take a stroll through Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Enjoy panoramic views of Anamudi Peak, Kerala’s highest mountain.

Thekkady – The Wildlife Sanctuary
Next, head to Thekkady, known for its pristine Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Indulge in a thrilling boat safari on Periyar Lake, where you can spot wild elephants, tigers, and various species of birds. Explore the spice plantations and immerse yourself in the fragrance of cardamom, pepper, and vanilla.

Alleppey – The Backwater Paradise
Discover the serene beauty of Alleppey as you cruise through its tranquil backwaters on a traditional houseboat. Glide past lush landscapes, quaint villages, and swaying coconut trees. Enjoy the local delicacies and immerse yourself in the peaceful rhythm of life along the backwaters.

Trivandrum – The Cultural Hub
Visit Trivandrum, Kerala’s capital city, known for its rich cultural heritage. Explore the Padmanabhaswamy Temple, an architectural marvel, and visit the Napier Museum to see an extensive collection of ancient artifacts. Take a relaxing walk on Kovalam Beach and experience Kerala’s vibrant coastal lifestyle.

Kanyakumari – The Land’s End
End your journey at Kanyakumari, where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet at the southern tip of India. Witness the mesmerizing sunset and sunrise views from the Vivekananda Rock Memorial and visit the iconic Thiruvalluvar Statue. The spiritual atmosphere of Kanyakumari will leave you in awe.

Start your journey in Munnar, where mist-covered mountains and lush tea plantations set a peaceful tone. Thekkady – The Wildlife Sanctuary
Next, visit Thekkady, home to the renowned Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Enjoy a boat safari on Periyar Lake, where you can spot wild elephants, tigers, and a wide array of bird species. Alleppey – The Backwater Paradise
Conclude your journey with a visit to Alleppey, also known as the Venice of the East. Glide along the serene backwaters on a traditional houseboat, passing picturesque landscapes of lush greenery, swaying palms, and tranquil villages. Experience the unique lifestyle of Kerala’s backwaters, relax, and enjoy the authentic flavors of local Kerala cuisine onboard.
