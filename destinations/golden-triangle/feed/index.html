This classic Indian tour combines rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture in three of India’s most iconic cities.

Delhi – The Capital City
Start your adventure in Delhi, a city that seamlessly blends ancient heritage with modernity. Visit the majestic Red Fort, explore the intricate beauty of Jama Masjid, and discover the serene Qutub Minar. Don’t miss a visit to the India Gate and Lotus Temple for a true taste of Delhi’s charm.

Agra – The City of Love
Next, travel to Agra, home to the magnificent Taj Mahal, a symbol of eternal love. Explore the historic Agra Fort, and visit the serene Mehtab Bagh for stunning views of the Taj Mahal. Agra’s rich Mughal history and architecture will leave you in awe.

Jaipur – The Pink City
End your journey in the royal city of Jaipur, where the grandeur of Rajasthan comes alive. Visit the stunning Amber Fort, the iconic Hawa Mahal, and the intricate City Palace. Explore the bustling Johari Bazaar and indulge in local crafts and cuisine.
