This comprehensive journey takes you through the magnificent cities of Jaipur, Pushkar, Chittorgarh, Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer, where each city is an embodiment of majestic forts, rich history, vibrant culture, and unforgettable experiences.

Jaipur – The Pink City
Start your royal journey in the heart of Rajasthan at Jaipur, the land of vibrant markets and grand palaces. Explore the iconic Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and the Jantar Mantar observatory. Don’t miss a traditional Rajasthani dinner at Chokhi Dhani for a taste of local culture.

Pushkar – The Sacred Town
Visit the holy town of Pushkar, home to the only Brahma Temple in the world. Stroll around the Pushkar Lake, and enjoy the colorful markets offering everything from traditional handicrafts to souvenirs. Feel the peace of this divine destination.

Chittorgarh – The Land of Valor
Experience the grandeur of Chittorgarh Fort, one of India’s largest and most impressive forts. Learn about the saga of Rajput bravery through the fort’s temples, palaces, and towers, and witness the beautiful Rana Kumbha Palace and Vijay Stambh (Tower of Victory).

Udaipur – The City of Lakes
Discover the serenity of Udaipur, where stunning lakes meet palatial architecture. Explore the City Palace, Jag Mandir, and take a boat ride on Lake Pichola for magnificent views. Udaipur’s royal gardens and temples will make you feel like you’ve stepped into a fairy tale.

Jodhpur – The Blue City
Feel the soul of Rajasthan in Jodhpur, with its striking blue-painted houses and majestic forts. Visit the Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan Palace, and Clock Tower. Don’t miss the bustling Sardar Market, where you can shop for traditional textiles, spices, and jewelry.

Jaisalmer – The Golden City
End your royal journey in the mesmerizing Jaisalmer, known for its golden sandstone architecture. Explore the majestic Jaisalmer Fort, the Patwon Ki Haveli, and the stunning Sam Sand Dunes. A camel ride through the Thar Desert offers a quintessential Rajasthan experience.
