This tour blends divine devotion, ancient history, and scenic tranquility — perfect for families, pilgrims, and history lovers alike.

Bhubaneshwar – The Temple City
Witness centuries of architectural grandeur at Lingaraj Temple, Mukteshwar Temple, and Rajarani Temple. Discover Odisha’s art, heritage, and soulful traditions.

Puri – The Abode of Lord Jagannath
Experience the divine aura of Shree Jagannath Temple, stroll along Puri Beach, and visit the sacred Gundicha Temple and Loknath Temple.

Konark – The Sun Temple Marvel
A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Konark Sun Temple showcases the brilliance of ancient Indian architecture and sculpture.

Diamond Triangle – Ratnagiri, Lalitgiri & Udayagiri
Explore the ancient Buddhist monasteries, stupas, and excavation sites forming the “Diamond Triangle” — a serene experience connecting spirituality with history.
