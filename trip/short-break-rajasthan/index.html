ROYAL RAJASTHAN : SHORT BREAK RAJASTHAN

Experience the essence of Rajasthan in a short yet memorable getaway that blends heritage, culture, and charm. Explore the vibrant streets and majestic palaces of Jaipur, the Pink City, with its iconic Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. Take a walk through colorful bazaars filled with handicrafts and traditional Rajasthani flavors. Discover the serenity of Pushkar, home to the sacred Brahma Temple and holy lake, where spirituality meets cultural vibrance. This short break offers a glimpse into Rajasthan’s royal legacy, colorful traditions, and warm hospitality—perfect for travelers seeking a quick royal escape.

Mewar Marwad

Day 01:-Jaipur-Arrival
As you arrive at Jaipur Railway station, transferred to your hotel. Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan state. Jaipur is one of the few planned cities in India. Upon arrival, Check-in into the hotel. , visit an architectural delight Hawa Mahal (the wind palace); experience the astrology at Jantar Mantar (the observatory), The Royal City Palace & its Museum Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 02:-Jaipur City Tour
After breakfast at the hotel proceed to half day city tour of one of most famous architecture Amber Fort and Jal Mahal spend some time in Jaipur local market Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 03:-Jaipur–Ajmer-Pushkar:-[140 KMS/02 ½ HRS]
After breakfast proceed further to auspicious place Ajmer & Pushkar, En-route stop at the Dargah Sharif Ajmer. Upon arrival, Check-in into the hotel. Later visit only Brahma Temple of the world and holy Pushkar Lake. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 04:-Pushkar-Chittore-Udaipur:-[340 KMS/06 HRS]
After breakfast, take a drive to city of Lake’s Udaipur, En-route stop at the indomitable price of Chittaur/Chittorgarh its fort, In fort we can see here Victory Tower, (Vijay Stambh), the tower of fame (KirtiStambh) and Padmini’s place. Finally arrive at Udaipur, check in to the hotel. Rest of the time is kept free to experience the natural beauty of this lake town. Dinner & Stay overnight at the hotel.
Day 05:-Udaipur City Tour:-
The day starts with a visit to the Jagdish Temple, a large Hindu temple made in the Indo-Aryan style of architecture.
The City Palace also adorns your itinerary. Your next destination is Saheliyon Ki Bari, Bhartiy Lok Kala Mandal, Maharana Pratap Smarak ,Udaipur City Palace . After some moments of relaxation Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 06:-Udaipur-Haldi Ghati-Ranakpur-Jodhpur:-[260 KMS/05 ½ hrs]
After breakfast drive to Blue City, Jodhpur. En-route stop at the famous battle ground Haldi Ghati where the war was fought in 1576 AD between Hero of Mewar Maharana Pratap and the troops of Mughal Emperor Akbar. later visit Ranakpur Jain temple Master class of india Architecture after that proceed to Jodhpur visit Umed Bhavab and Jaswant Thada check-in into hotel Stay overnight at Jodhpur hotel.
Day 07:-Jodhpur-Jaisalmer Sams Duns :-[280 KMS/06 ½ hrs]
After breakfast visit Mehrangarh Fort flaunting intricate Rajasthani carvings and design. later proceed the city with the most majestic wonders of history and cultures. En-route you will stop at the Jaisalmer War museum. Later continue your drive to Jaisalmer. Upon arrival, check in into the Desert Camp Here you will also enjoy Sam Sand Dunes for a remarkable Thar Desert safari experience at the sunset. The safari will end with a traditional dinner and Kalbeliya Dance performance. Camp for an overnight stay.
Day 08:-Jaisalmer-Full Day sightseeing Hotel Stay
After breakfast, get ready for a day tour of Jaisalmer. First at Gadsisar Lake also known as Gadisar Lake. Later, start the city tour by exploring the exquisite Jain temples in Golden Fort having both religious as well as historical significance. Visit some of the finest Havelis of Jaisalmer as Patwon Ki Haveli, Nathmalji Ki Haveli, and Salim Singh Ji Ki Haveli. The walking through the traditional markets of Jaisalmer looking at the old houses and monuments will be an unforgettable experience.
Day 9:-Drop for Return Journey
After Breakfast Drop at Jaisalmer Railway station or Airport tour end with Sweet memories

1. What are the key highlights of this tour?

Jaipur: Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar, local bazaars
Pushkar (optional): Brahma Temple, Pushkar Lake, colorful streets.

2. What should I pack for the trip?

Light cottons in summer, light woolens in winter Comfortable footwear for fort visits & walking in bazaars Sunglasses, cap, sunscreen, camera.

3. Is this package suitable for families and kids?

Yes, Rajasthan is family-friendly. Children especially enjoy fort visits, camel rides, and colorful markets.

4. What type of accommodation will be provided?

You will stay in neat and comfortable hotels/resorts with modern amenities. Heritage/luxury hotel upgrades are available on request.

5. Are cultural activities included in this package?

Cultural shows, folk dance performances, and traditional Rajasthani dinners are optional but can be arranged at an additional cost.
